## 🌾 The Problem

India has **140+ million farmers**. Every year, **15–25% of crops are lost** to diseases — that's billions worth of food destroyed. The main challenge:

- Farmers in rural areas have **no quick access to agricultural experts**
- By the time they get advice, the disease has **spread to the entire field**
- This leads to **massive crop loss, debt, and food insecurity**

## 💡 Our Solution

**Crop AI** is a free, browser-based tool that lets any farmer instantly detect crop diseases from a photo.

```
📸 Upload leaf photo
        ↓
🤖 AI analyzes disease patterns
        ↓
✅ Get diagnosis + treatment + prevention
```

No app download. No login. Works on any phone.

## 🦠 Diseases Covered (38+)

<details>
<summary>Click to see full disease list</summary>

### 🌿 General Disease Types
- Viral Disease (Mosaic / Yellow patches)
- Fungal Disease (Spots / Mold / Rust)
- Pest Damage (Holes / Chewed edges)
- Early Blight (Brown target rings)
- Late Blight (Dark wet patches — URGENT)
- Leaf Gall / Blister (Bumps on leaf)
- Black Spot / Dark Patches
- Bacterial Spot (Water-soaked spots)

### 🍎 Apple — Seb ka Ped
- Apple Rust, Apple Scab, Apple Black Rot, Healthy

### 🍒 Cherry & 🫐 Blueberry
- Cherry Powdery Mildew, Healthy

### 🍊 Orange / Citrus — Narangi
- Huanglongbing (Citrus Greening Disease)

### 🌽 Corn / Maize — Makka
- Gray Leaf Spot, Common Rust, Northern Leaf Blight, Healthy

### 🍇 Grape — Angoor ki Bel
- Black Rot, Black Measles (Esca), Leaf Blight, Healthy

### 🍑 Peach — Aadoo
- Bacterial Spot, Healthy

### 🌶️ Pepper / Capsicum — Mirch
- Bacterial Spot, Healthy

### 🥔 Potato — Aloo
- Early Blight, Late Blight

### 🫘 Soybean & 🎃 Squash / Kaddu
- Soybean Healthy, Squash Powdery Mildew

### 🍓 Strawberry
- Leaf Scorch, Healthy

### 🍅 Tomato — Tamatar
- Bacterial Spot, Early Blight, Spider Mites, Leaf Mould, Septoria Spot
